Who We Are

We’re two creatives who turned our shared passion for creating unforgettable moments into a business built on creativity, connection, and care. With an eye for detail and a heart for design, we founded Zariyah to bring fresh ideas and seamless execution to every event. From weddings and birthdays to corporate gatherings and community celebrations, we believe every event should tell a story that reflects the people behind it. Together, we combine our vision, skills, and dedication to make sure no detail is overlooked and every moment shines. At Zariyah, you’re not just hiring planners—you’re partnering with two women committed to making your celebration stress-free, stylish, and truly memorable.
